I am a PhD candidate in the Regional Climate Group at the Department for Earth Sciences,University of Gothenburg My PhD project is about past and future changes in the atmospheric water cycle in the Tibetan Plateau and Himalaya regions with a specific focus on organized convection and extreme precipitation. My aim is to improve our understanding of the physical processes that lead to extreme precipitation, because this is a pre-requisite to robustly assess future changes in the regional climate. I also try to bridge the gap between observational and modeling perspectives, so I am working with both ground-based and space-borne observations as well as numerical weather and climate models.
