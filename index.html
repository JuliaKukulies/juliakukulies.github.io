I am a PhD candidate in the Regional Climate Group at the Department for Earth Sciences, University of Gothenburg (Sweden). I am doing climate research and with a Master's degree in Atmospheric Sciences, I am mainly interested in the question how a warmer climate is changing atmospheric processes that control heavy rain and water availability, because these parameters are essential for how we build our future societies.

While my research focuses on the physical aspects of climate change, I am also interested in questions related to sustainability transitions and climate justice. I am part of a blogg collective where me and my fellow bloggers write about these topics - from commonplace everyday life tips and concerns to reflections about the bigger questions of climate vulnerability and adaption(sorry, so far only in Swedish).
