About Me
---

I am currently an ASP Postdoctoral Fellow at the NSF National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) in Boulder, Colorado, working in the Capacity Center for Climate & Weather Extremes section. I hold a Ph.D. in Atmospheric Sciences, and my research focuses on **precipitation changes** in the context of climate change. Understanding how the nature, frequency, and intensity of precipitation evolve in a warming climate is critical for building resilient societies, as these changes directly impact water resources and natural hazards like floods and droughts.
